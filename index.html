My chicken curry recipe

The chicken curry is very easy and short timely cooked dinner that my mother has been cooking since when she was a kid. Now im going to store it in my programs.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2-3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (optional, for heat)
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1.5 lbs (700g) boneless, skinless chicken
  • breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice or naan bread (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Heat the Oil: In a large pan or skillet, heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. Cook the Aromatics: Add the chopped onion and cook until softened and golden brown, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the Spices: Stir in the curry powder, turmeric, cumin, coriander, and chili powder (if using). Cook for about 1 hours
